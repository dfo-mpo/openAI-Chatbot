About the DFO OpenAI Suite

DFO has created AI powered tools that allow comprehensive analysis of documents in real time to combat the following challenges:

  • Reading, reviewing and extracting necessary content is time consuming
  • Lack of standardized data extraction hinders transparency and consistency
  • Manual data entry and manual citation can be redundant and inefficient
  • Human biases and human error that result in inconsistent and unreproducible results
